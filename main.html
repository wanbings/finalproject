Riser

Galaxy Crescent (Beginner)

The Galaxy Crescent is one of the cheapest ILF risers on the market. It has all the necessary accessory mounts and is lightweight at 2.01 lbs, making it ideal for younger archers. It is a great entry level choice. However, the paint scratches easily and the bow will need to be replaced as it is not made to last long-term. It is constructed from magnesium.

Cartel Fathom (Beginner)

As one of the most affordable risers available, the Fathom has a sleek, modern design. It has all the necessary accessory mounts and will be a sturdier beginner riser at 2.6 lbs. However, this weight may be too heavy for younger archers. As an entry level choice, it can only handle limbs up to 36 lbs and will soon need to be upgraded. It is constructed from cast aluminum.

Hoyt Xakt (Intermediate)

From one of the most notable brands in archery, the Hoyt Xakt is ideal for intermediate archers. It features the technology of professional Hoyt risers, such as Dynamic Flex Control, for a cheaper price. It weighs in at 2.38 lbs. This bow is ideal for those who want a popular name brand that will likely last them for the rest of their recreational archery career.

Mybo Wave XR (Intermediate)

Made in the UK, this bow is good for those who have parts that use the metric system. The riser has an offset design for improved lateral balance. It comes in over 8 beautiful anodised colors. The riser weighs in at 2.5 pounds and is constructed from 6082 aluminum. It is a good choice for any archers looking for an aesthetic bow that will provide a solid performance in the long run.

Win&Win WIAWIS ATF-DX (Pro)

From one of the most notable brands in archery, the Win&Win WIAWIS is one of the highest quality bows on the market. It has a Mathews EHS dampening system to improve shot feeling and accuracy. It weighs in at 3 pounds and is constructed from CNC Machined Aluminum. It is recommended for experienced and pro archers who cannot sacrifice any points during competitions. It is not recommended inexperienced archers purchase this, as it is not required at their level and it is extremely pricey.

Hoyt GMX 3 Series (Pro)

Used by internationally ranked archers, this bow will ensure a smooth shot. Unlike the other Hoyt risers, this riser can accept ILF limbs like other risers. It features the Dynamic Balance System, Perfect Balance Weight System, and Dynamic Flex Control, all made to improve your shot feeling. The GMX has a high quality construction of premium stainless steel, weighing in at 2.93 lbs. The bow is targeted for those who are looking to compete with the best of the best. Otherwise, there are other cheaper risers that can fulfill your needs.
