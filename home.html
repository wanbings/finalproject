Galaxy Crescent (Beginner)

The Galaxy Crescent is one of the cheapest ILF risers on the market. It has all the necessary accessory mounts and is lightweight at 2.01 lbs, making it ideal for younger archers. It is a great entry level choice. However, the paint scratches easily and the bow will need to be replaced as it is not made to last long-term.
